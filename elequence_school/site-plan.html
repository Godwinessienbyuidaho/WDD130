Enrollment / payment. To enroll your child in any of our unique schools and give your child the opportunity to grow in wisdom, knowledge and statue, visit any of our schools near you, fill out the form and your child is good to go. Payment of tuition. In eloquence schools you can make your payment in cash or through bank transfer. If you are making a cash payment remember you do not have to worry about making full payment at a time you can pay by installment. When paying by installment, note that the deadline for all payment is at the middle of the term. If by the middle of the term your child tuition has not been paid in full, your child stand a chance of losing his or her privilege as a student in our esteemed school. Payment for learning and workshop materials. Please note that in Eloquence School, payment for learning and workshop materials comes first. What this means that no matter the mode of tuition payment for a child, the payment that parents make firstly is for the workshop and learning materials. No tuition will be accepted only until the workshop and learning materials has been paid for in full.
