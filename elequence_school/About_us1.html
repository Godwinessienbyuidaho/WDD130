Welcome to Eloquence Schools

This page will briefly tell you what we do and how we operate. So how then do we operate? In eloquence schools we offer firstly, general courses that helps your kid to read and write and to know more about his environment and secondly, courses that will allow your kids to expand, develop their talent and do more with the knowledge gained from the general course.

Enrollment / Materials.

To enroll your child in any of our unique schools and give your child the opportunity to grow in wisdom, knowledge and statue, visit any of our schools near you, fill out the form and your child is good to go. Here in Eloquence schools we have all the material it would take for your child to successfully go through with our program. We have trained fashion designers who are prepared to tailor your child’s uniform in a just a very unique way that stands out and makes your child comfortable in class and during workshops and practices. We do not allow parent to get learning materials elsewhere so as to avoid the purchase of outdated or sub-substandard learning materials. So we have all the text, reading and writing materials including workshop and practice materials for your kid.

Tuition / payment.

In eloquence schools we’ve got you covered in terms of tuition so you don’t have to be bothered. We have plans that allows you to pay for your child’s tuition installmentally. This way you have more time to pay and less money to pay at a time. Payment of tuition. In eloquence schools you can make your payment in cash or through bank transfer. If you are making a cash payment remember you do not have to worry about making full payment at a time you can pay by installment. When paying by installment, note that the deadline for all payment is at the middle of the term. If by the middle of the term your child tuition has not been paid in full, your child stand a chance of losing his or her privilege as a student in our esteemed school. Payment for learning and workshop materials. Please note that in Eloquence School, payment for learning and workshop materials comes first. What this means that no matter the mode of tuition payment for a child, the payment that parents make firstly is for the workshop and learning materials. No tuition will be accepted only until the workshop and learning materials has been paid for in full.
